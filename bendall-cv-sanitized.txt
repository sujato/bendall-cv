atha kho āyasmā ānando ārāmaṁ gaṁtvā bhikkhūnaṁ etam atthaṁ ārocesuṁsi ||
bhagavato ||
anujānāmi bhikkhave gaṇṭhikaṁ pāsakanti ||
tena kho pana samayena chavvaggiyā bhikkhu uccāvāca gaṇṭhikāyo dhārenti sovaṇṇāyaṁ rūpimayaṁ |
manussā ujjhayanti khiyyanti vipacenti
seyyathāpi gihi kāmabhoginoti ||
bhagavato |
na bhikkhave uccāvācā gaṇṭhikā dhāretavvā
yo dhāreyya āpatti dukkaṭassa ||
anujānāmi bhikkhave aṭṭhimayaṁ dantamayaṁvisaṇamayaṁ | dantamayaṁ || veḍḍumayaṁ kaṭṭhamayaṁ| jatumayaṁ| phalamayaṁ|| lohamayaṁ saṁkhanābhimayaṁ | suttamayanti|| pka || tha || pka ||

cīvaraṁ jīrati ||
bhagavato |
anujānāmi bhikkhave gaṇṭhikaphalakaṁ pāsakāphalikaṁti ||
gaṇṭhikaphalakaṁ pi pāsakaphalakaṁ pi anti appenti
kāḍḍo vivariyati
bhagavato ||
anujānāmi bhikkhave gaṇṭhikaphalakaṁ pasakaphalakanti | gaṇṭhikaphalakaṁ pi pāsikaphalakaṁ pi ante appenti koḍḍo vivariyati
bhagavato |
anujanāmi bhikkhave gaṇṭhikaphalakaṁ ante appetuṁ pāsakaphalakaṁ sattaṁguliṁ vā aṭṭhaṁguliṁ vā ogahetvā appetunti || tha ||

manussā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti
seyyathāpi gihi kāmabhoginoti
bhagavato etam atthaṁ ārocesuṁ
na bhikkhave gihinivatthaṁ nivāsetavvaṁ |

yo nivāseyya āpatti dukkaṭassāti || pka || tha || pka ||
tena kho pana samayena chavvaggiyā bhikkhu gihiparutaṁ pārupaṁti
manussā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti
seyyathāpi gihi kāmabhoginoti |
bhagavato etam atthaṁ ārocesuṁ |
na bhikkhave gihipārutaṁ pārupitavvaṁ
yo pārupeyya āpatti dukkaṭassāti || pka || tha || pka ||

seyyathāpi raṁño muṇḍaveṭṭīti ||
bhagavato etam atthaṁ arocesuṁ
na bhikkhave saṁveliyaṁ nivāsetavvaṁ
yo nivāseyya āpatti dukkaṭassāti || pka || tha || pka ||

manussa ujjhayanti khiyyanti vipācenti
seyyathāpi raṁño muṇḍavaṭṭīti
bhagavato ||
na bhikkhave ubhatokācaṁ haritavvaṁ
yo hareyya āpatti dukkaṭassa ||
anujānāmi bhikkhave ekatokācaṁ antarakācaṁ sīsābhāraṁ | khandhabhāraṁ kaṭibhāraṁ olaṁvakan ti|| pka || tha || pka ||

mukhaṁ duggandhaṁ hoti |
bhagavato etam atthaṁ ārocesuṁ |

bhagavato ||

yo ākoṭeyya āpatti dukkaṭassāti || pka || tha || pka ||
